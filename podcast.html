Epic. The Podcast.

Hey all and thanks for expressing interest in my podcast. My "show" combines great music of all genres, from the delta-blues on the 1940s to Spoon's great new album, I try and keep a good mix while maintaining a good balance so the music does't sound "all over the place." I've recently been trying to include a few installments to the show, that we would do every episode. Our first, being the Buddhist-Minute, seemed to get some good feedback, as did the "Iggy is an Asshole" segment, where we always play something that is Jack White-related. I do this becuase Iggy told me that I play too much Jack White on the show. So go head and press play before you regret your decision and it turns into one of those "it's so bad that it's actually good."
